## Brand & Style

今心 (ImXin) is an emotional-awareness companion app built on the Yale RULER framework. Its design philosophy draws from two core ideas: the name itself —「今心即為念」("today's heart is mind") — and the concept of a "luminous sanctuary" where users pause, recognize, and regulate their emotions.

The visual identity is **Luminous Morandi**: a softened, muted color palette inspired by Giorgio Morandi's still-life paintings, translated into digital space. Unlike typical wellness apps that rely on bright, saturated primaries, 今心 uses desaturated, earthy tones that feel gentle, non-judgmental, and contemplative. The interface whispers rather than shouts, creating a safe emotional container.

The aesthetic sits between **minimal glassmorphism** and **warm materiality**. Glass surfaces are extremely subtle — barely-there frost rather than crystalline refraction — allowing the deep charcoal (dark) or warm parchment (light) backgrounds to breathe. Elevation is created through tonal shifts and soft shadows rather than harsh borders, giving the interface a floating, weightless quality.

## Colors

The color system is organized around two hierarchies: **emotional** and **functional**.

### Emotional Palette: The Mood Quadrant

The four quadrant colors are the soul of the interface. They map directly to the RULER emotional framework:

- **Red (#C58B8A)** — High Energy, Low Pleasantness. Anger, anxiety, stress. Used for SOS mode, crisis indicators, and high-intensity emotional states.
- **Yellow (#D5C1A5)** — High Energy, High Pleasantness. Excitement, joy, anticipation. Used for primary actions, celebrations, and active states.
- **Blue (#97A6B4)** — Low Energy, Low Pleasantness. Sadness, fatigue, melancholy. Used for coach interactions, breathing exercises, and contemplative flows.
- **Green (#AAB09B)** — Low Energy, High Pleasantness. Calm, contentment, peace. Used for completion states, regulation success, and grounding moments.

In light mode, these colors shift to slightly more saturated variants to maintain contrast against the warm cream background.

### Functional Palette

- **Background/Surface**: Deep charcoal (#1a1a1a) in dark mode, warm parchment (#f5f3ef) in light mode. The surface layer (#242424 / #ebe7e0) provides subtle separation without visual heaviness.
- **Text**: Primary text uses near-white (#e8e8e8) in dark mode and near-black (#2d2d2d) in light mode. Secondary text is always noticeably softer to establish clear information hierarchy.
- **Glass**: The glass system uses extremely low alpha values — `hsla(0, 0%, 100%, 0.025)` for backgrounds and `0.08` for borders in dark mode. In light mode, this inverts to black-based transparency (`hsla(0, 0%, 0%, 0.04)`). This ensures the glass effect is felt but never dominates.
- **Gradients**: Each quadrant has a matching radial gradient that creates an "aura" effect — a soft glow emanating from emotional touchpoints. These gradients fade from the solid Morandi tone to near-transparent at 70% radius.

### Theme Strategy

Dark mode is the default and primary experience. Light mode is a fully inverted system where all alpha channels swap polarity (white-transparent becomes black-transparent) to preserve the same relative contrast relationships. A 0.5s ease transition connects the two states smoothly.

## Typography

The type system pairs **Inter** with **Noto Sans TC** to support Traditional Chinese text natively. System fonts serve as fallbacks for instant rendering.

- **Display (28px / 800)**: Reserved for the app logo and major emotional state headlines. Uses tight negative tracking for a modern, confident presence.
- **Headline Large (24px / 700)**: Section titles, SOS headers, and completion celebrations.
- **Headline Medium (20px / 600)**: Card titles, scenario names, and step headers within flows.
- **Title Large (18px / 600)**: Sub-section headings and emphasized labels.
- **Body Large (16px / 400)**: Primary reading text. Comfortable line-height (1.5) for emotional content that users may read slowly.
- **Body Medium (14px / 400)**: Secondary descriptions, metadata, and chat message text.
- **Label Medium (14px / 600)**: Buttons, navigation links, and interactive labels.
- **Label Small (12px / 500)**: Timestamps, badges, and micro-copy. Uses positive letter-spacing for readability at small sizes.

All text is rendered with `-webkit-font-smoothing: antialiased` for crisp edges on mobile displays.

## Layout & Spacing

The layout follows a **mobile-first, single-column container** model optimized for emotional touchpoints.

- **Container**: A centered column with max-width 600px (mobile) → 760px (tablet) → 920px (desktop). On very large screens, a subtle 100px diffuse shadow and 1px glass borders create the illusion of a floating card.
- **Base Grid**: 4px base unit. All spacing derives from this atomic value.
- **Scale**: 4px, 8px, 12px, 16px, 20px, 24px, 32px, 40px, 48px. This tight, granular scale allows precise emotional rhythm — generous whitespace for breathing room, tight gaps for related elements.
- **Content Padding**: 16px horizontal on mobile, expanding to 24px and 32px on larger screens.
- **Safe Areas**: `env(safe-area-inset-bottom)` is respected for fixed input bars and floating action buttons, ensuring the UI never conflicts with device gestures.

## Elevation & Depth

Depth in 今心 is achieved through **attenuated glass layers** and **chromatic shadows** rather than traditional Material-style elevation.

### The Glass Stack

- **Level 0 (Base)**: Solid background with an optional radial aura gradient at the top (using the current emotional quadrant color at 12% opacity).
- **Level 1 (Standard Glass)**: `backdrop-filter: blur(24px)`, `background: hsla(0, 0%, 100%, 0.025)`, `border: 1px solid hsla(0, 0%, 100%, 0.08)`. Used for cards, input fields, and chat bubbles.
- **Level 2 (Elevated)**: `background: hsla(0, 0%, 100%, 0.06)` (surface-elevated), same blur and border. Used for hover states, selected items, and modal cards.
- **Level 3 (Mask)**: `background: hsla(0, 0%, 0%, 0.6)` with `backdrop-filter: blur(12px)`. Used for modal overlays and crisis interventions. The darker mask creates emotional gravity appropriate for serious moments.

### Shadows

The `shadow-luxe` token is a three-layer composition:
1. A tight, dark layer for immediate separation (`0 4px 24px -1px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.2)`)
2. A broad, soft layer for atmospheric depth (`0 10px 40px -5px rgba(0, 0, 0, 0.3)`)
3. An inset 1px highlight for glass edge definition (`inset 0 0 0 1px hsla(0, 0%, 100%, 0.05)`)

In light mode, shadow intensities drop by roughly 60% to maintain subtlety against the lighter canvas.

## Shapes

The shape language is **generously rounded** to evoke softness, safety, and approachability.

- **Luxe (32px)**: Hero cards, summary containers, modal dialogs, and celebration states. Creates a pillowy, welcoming embrace.
- **Large (24px)**: Primary buttons, scenario cards, and major interactive surfaces.
- **Medium (16px)**: Standard cards, input fields, and list items. The workhorse radius.
- **Small (12px)**: Navigation buttons, compact chips, and secondary actions.
- **Full (9999px)**: Avatars, floating action buttons, SOS buttons, and circular breathing guides. Used for elements that should feel organic and touchable.

Chat bubbles use asymmetric radii (`20px 20px 4px 20px` for model, `20px 20px 20px 4px` for user) to create a subtle directional cue without relying on tails.

## Motion

Motion in 今心 is **slow, smooth, and emotionally resonant**. Fast, snappy animations would feel jarring in an emotional-regulation context.

- **Luxe Transition**: `0.5s cubic-bezier(0.23, 1, 0.32, 1)` — the default for all interactive states. An ease-out-quint curve that decelerates gently, feeling like a sigh rather than a snap.
- **Standard Transition**: `0.4s ease` — for simpler property changes like color and border.
- **Theme Transition**: `0.5s ease` for background-color and color — allows the eye to adapt without disorientation.
- **Bounce**: `cubic-bezier(0.34, 1.56, 0.64, 1)` — reserved exclusively for celebration moments (completion confetti, streak badges) to inject a brief moment of joy.

### Key Animations

- **fadeInUp**: Content enters by rising 10px while fading in over 0.5s. Used for page transitions and step reveals.
- **breatheLuxe**: A 3-second scale-and-opacity cycle (1.0→1.8, 0.3→0.7) with `cubic-bezier(0.4, 0, 0.2, 1)`. Powers the centering circle during emotional grounding.
- **confettiBurst**: Particles burst upward then fall with rotation, using the current quadrant color. A 1.2s celebration animation.
- **glow-pulse**: A breathing light effect that increases brightness and blur size, used for ambient emotional indicators.

All animations respect `prefers-reduced-motion: reduce`, either disabling decorative motion or slowing functional animations (breathing guides extend from 3s to 12s).

## Components

### Glass Cards

The foundational container. Every glass card uses the standard glass background with a 24px blur, 1px border, and luxe shadow. Content sits inside with comfortable padding (24px–32px). On hover, cards may lift 2px with an increased shadow, but never change background color drastically — the glass integrity must be preserved.

### Mood Quadrant Grid

A 2×2 grid of large touch targets representing the four emotional quadrants. Each cell uses `surface-elevated` as a base, with a 2px transparent border that transitions to the quadrant's color on hover. The hover state also lifts the card 4px. On selection, the border locks to the quadrant color and the background subtly deepens.

### Buttons

- **Primary (Solid)**: Filled with `on-background` color (near-white in dark mode), text in `background` color. Bold weight, letter-spacing of 1px for a confident label. Hover lifts 2px and applies the luxe shadow.
- **Outline**: Transparent background, 1px glass border, secondary text color. Rounded to a full pill (30px). Hover brings the border and text to primary color.
- **Gradient**: Used sparingly for SOS actions and timer buttons. Combines two Morandi tones in a 135° linear gradient, with text in dark charcoal for contrast.

### Input Fields

Text inputs use `surface-input` background with a glass border. On focus, the border transitions to quadrant-blue with a 3px soft glow (`rgba(151, 166, 180, 0.15)`). Placeholder text uses secondary color at 60% opacity. The rounded corner matches the medium (16px) token.

### Chat Bubbles

The AI Coach conversation uses an asymmetric bubble system:
- **User bubbles**: Gradient-blue fill, dark text, sharp bottom-left corner. A soft blue shadow connects the message to the user identity.
- **Coach bubbles**: Glass fill with blur, bordered, sharp bottom-right corner. This makes the coach feel integrated into the environment rather than overlaid.
- **Avatars**: 36px circles with quadrant-gradient backgrounds. The user avatar uses blue-gradient; the coach uses yellow-gradient.

### Chips & Tags

Option chips for emotional labels and scenario tags use the glass background with a pill shape (30px radius). Selected states invert to solid `on-background` with `background`-colored text. This high-contrast inversion creates satisfying tactile feedback.

### Floating Action Button (FAB)

A 56px circular button fixed to the bottom-right. Uses the blue-gradient with luxe shadow. Hover scales up 5% and lifts; active scales down 5%. The FAB provides immediate access to the AI Coach from any screen.

### Modal Overlays

Crisis modals and resume prompts use the mask surface (`hsla(0, 0%, 0%, 0.6)`) with a 12px blur. The modal card itself uses the elevated surface with a luxe border-radius and warm-tinted border for crisis contexts (e.g., `hsla(30, 50%, 60%, 0.3)` for SOS mode).

### Breathing Visualizer

A central circular element (128px–180px) with a blue radial gradient. It scales rhythmically (0.85× to 1.25×) over a 7-second cycle synchronized to the breathing guidance. A diffuse blue shadow pulses in sync, creating an immersive, hypnotic focal point for emotional regulation.
